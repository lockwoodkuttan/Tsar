TSAR LINEN - PRODUCT IMAGES
===========================

Drop your product photos in this folder.

NAMING CONVENTION (very important):
- classic-crew.jpg     → Classic Crew product
- heavyweight.jpg      → Heavyweight Tee product
- custom.jpg           → Custom Branded product
- polo.jpg             → Polo product
- longsleeve.jpg       → Long Sleeve product
- linen.jpg            → Eco Linen Blend product

SPECS:
- Format: JPG or PNG (JPG preferred, smaller file size)
- Size: 800x1000 pixels recommended (3:4 ratio looks best)
- File size: keep under 500KB each (use tinypng.com to compress)

After uploading the images and committing on GitHub, your site will
automatically use them in place of the placeholder SVG illustrations.

How It Works

From inquiry to delivered.

No mystery, no middlemen, no surprise charges. Here's exactly what happens when you place an order with Tsar Linen.

I
Day 0–1

Request a Quote

Tell us what you need — quantity, fit, color, branding, deadline. Use our quote form, send an email, or call. A real person on our team responds within one business day.

  • Delivered price (DDP) in USD, no surprise fees
  • Realistic lead time based on current capacity
  • Sample options and timing
II
Week 1–2

Approve a Sample

Before any large run, you get physical samples in your hands. Touch the fabric, check the fit, approve the print colors. We don't go to production until you're satisfied.

  • Stock samples shipped within 5 business days
  • Custom branded samples within 10 business days
  • Sample cost is credited back to your bulk order
III
Week 2–5

We Manufacture

Production runs at our partner facility in Tirupur or Ludhiana, depending on the product. Throughout production, we send progress photos, fabric inspection reports, and final QC documentation — full transparency from cut to pack.

  • 50% deposit at production start, 50% before shipment
  • Photo updates at cutting, sewing, and packing stages
  • Pre-shipment AQL 2.5 quality inspection on every order
IV
Week 5–6

Shipment & Customs

We handle customs brokerage, duties, and documentation through our US-based freight partners. You don't deal with CBP, HTS codes, or surprise import fees. The price we quoted is the price you pay.

  • Sea freight for orders 500+ units (cost-efficient)
  • Air freight available for urgent orders
  • All customs duties and fees included in the DDP price
V
Week 6+

Delivered to You

Your order arrives at your warehouse, office, or event venue. We follow up to ensure quality met expectations and to start the conversation about your reorder schedule.

  • Final-mile delivery via UPS, FedEx, or freight carrier
  • Photo documentation of the shipment leaving and arriving
  • 30-day quality guarantee — if anything's off, we make it right
Common Questions

Things buyers ask first.

What's your minimum order quantity?

50 units per design for stock products, 50 units per design for custom branded. We can sometimes accommodate smaller for new buyers — ask.

How long does an order really take?

Stock products: 4–5 weeks from order to delivery in the US. Custom branded: 5–6 weeks. Rush options available — contact us if you have a tight deadline.

What payment terms do you offer?

Standard terms are 50% deposit at production start, 50% before shipment. For repeat buyers with established history, we offer Net 30 on the second invoice.

Do you handle customs and import duties?

Yes. All quotes are DDP (Delivered Duty Paid). The price we quote includes shipping, customs brokerage, and import duties to your US delivery address.

What if there's a quality issue?

30-day quality guarantee. If anything doesn't match the approved sample, we replace it or refund — your choice. We document every order so disputes are rare.

Can I visit the manufacturing facility?

Absolutely. Serious buyers are welcome at our partner facilities in India. We'll coordinate the trip and tour. Most buyers do this once and never again — but the trust it builds is worth it.

Ready to begin?

Step one is the quote. Tell us what you need and we'll respond within one business day with a delivered price.

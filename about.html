Our Story

Three generations of thread.

Tsar Linen is a bridge — between American buyers who refuse to compromise on quality, and Indian craftsmen whose work has been hidden behind middlemen for too long.

The Beginning

A simple observation.

Tsar Linen was founded on a frustration we kept hearing from US buyers: they were paying boutique prices for commodity-quality apparel. Mass-produced tees marked up 4–5 times by the time they reached a corporate gift order or an event organizer's invoice.

Meanwhile, in Tirupur — the cotton-knit capital of India — family-run mills with three generations of expertise were producing world-class garments for European luxury brands, but had no way to reach American buyers directly.

We built that bridge. From skilled hands in India to a US-registered company that handles every order, every contract, every customer service call. Craftsmanship without the friction.

Our Network

Skilled hands, verified.

We don't own factories — we partner with the best ones. Every Tsar partner is family-operated, OEKO-TEX certified, and audited annually for fair labor practices and environmental compliance.

Our primary partners in Tirupur produce our cotton knits — t-shirts, polos, long sleeves. Our partners in Ludhiana produce our woven goods and heavier-weight pieces. Each order is matched to the partner whose specialty it is, not whoever has open capacity.

This isn't a marketplace where you might get any factory. Every Tsar order is traceable from yarn to your warehouse.

What We Stand For

Three principles, non-negotiable.

01

Transparency

You see your manufacturer. You see your factory. You see the inspection reports. No black boxes, no "trust us, it'll be fine."

02

Fair Labor

Every partner pays at or above the prevailing wage for skilled garment workers in their region. Annual third-party audits. Non-negotiable.

03

True Pricing

What we quote is what you pay — delivered, duties included. No surprise customs fees, no fuel surcharges, no last-minute "we ran into an issue" calls.

Serious buyers are welcome to tour our partner facilities in India. We'll arrange the trip. You see the operation firsthand.

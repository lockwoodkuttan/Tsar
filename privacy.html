Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Tsar Linen ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request or contact us, we collect:

  • Your name and company
  • Email address and (optionally) phone number
  • Information about your project — quantity, product preferences, deadlines, and any details you choose to share

We also automatically collect basic technical information when you visit the site (browser type, IP address, pages visited) — standard for any website.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Respond to your quote request
  • Send samples and pricing information you request
  • Communicate about your order if you become a customer
  • Improve our website and service quality

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We do not use your data for marketing campaigns or send unsolicited emails.

Sharing Your Information

We share your information only with:

  • Our manufacturing partners — only as needed to fulfill your order (e.g., shipping address)
  • Service providers we use to operate the site (e.g., Netlify for hosting, email providers)
  • Authorities when required by law

Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies — only what's necessary for the site to function. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

Data Retention

We keep quote requests and customer information for as long as needed to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of any communication from us

To exercise any of these rights, email lockwoodkuttan@gmail.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email lockwoodkuttan@gmail.com.

Note: This is a starter privacy policy. Once you have significant traffic or paying customers, consider having a lawyer review it for compliance with GDPR (EU customers), CCPA (California customers), and other applicable laws.
